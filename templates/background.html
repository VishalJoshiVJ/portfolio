Biography

Welcome! I am Vishal Joshi, a student studying Engineering in the branch of Computers Science in the Third year of degree.

Having born and brought up in the Belgaum city of Karnataka, I completed my education from nursery till my 10th from SKE Society's V M Shanbhag Marathi School, Bhagyanagar, Belgaum. I completed my 10th with 90.24% of percentage. After recognising my interest in the science field, I took the admission in one of the most reputed science college in Belgaum i.e. Govindram Seksaria Science College, Belgaum. I completed my PUC i.e. 12th with 89.5% of percentile and having interest in the Computer Science and Engineering, I completed the KCET exam immediately with ranking of 8734 and got admission in the current studying KLS Gogte Institute of Technology, Udyambag, Belgaum.

Other than studies, I have hobby in playing and watching cricket, travelling to various places, reading history books of India and I am constantly looking forward for opportunities to showcase my skillset and to keep on learning new things that are necessary to my overall growth. Thank You again.
